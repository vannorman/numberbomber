How To Play
  • Click a number tile to show its factors.
  • Click a factor to explode the tile.
  • When tiles explode, they explode neighbors IF they share a factor, OR if both are prime.
  • Win the level by clearing the board.
  • Chain together more explosions for more points!
  • Iced numbers must be melted before they can explode. Melt them by exploding their neighbors who share a factor.
  • Rocks cannot be exploded.
